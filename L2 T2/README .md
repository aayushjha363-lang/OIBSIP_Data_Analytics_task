## Project Overview

This project focuses on predicting the quality of wine samples using their chemical composition. By analyzing various physicochemical properties of wine, a **machine learning model** is developed to estimate the quality rating assigned to each sample. The goal is to understand how different chemical attributes influence the overall quality of wine.

## Dataset Description

The dataset consists of several chemical properties that contribute to wine characteristics:

* **Volatile Acidity** – Measures the amount of acetic acid present in the wine; excessive levels may result in an undesirable vinegar-like taste.
* **Citric Acid** – Contributes to the wine’s freshness and flavor balance.
* **Residual Sugar** – Represents the amount of sugar remaining after the fermentation process.
* **Sulphates** – Additives that can increase sulfur dioxide levels and help preserve the wine.
* **Alcohol** – Indicates the percentage of alcohol content in the wine.
* **Quality** – The target variable representing the wine quality score, typically ranging from 0 to 10.
